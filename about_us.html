TiNTiC was founded in St. George Utah in 2014. We didn't plan on becoming entrepreneurs, but after inventing the Clamp Saddle, TiNTiC was born. Other inventors quickly took notice of our product development processes and began asking us for help with their inventions.

TiNTiC Design is available to inventors of all experience levels. We can assist you in any aspect of your product development process. We have on a number of occasions assisted inventors beyond their capabilities and have added value to their inventions. We think it is very rewarding when we help inventors achieve their objectives.

TiNTiC Manufacturing specializes in production quantities ranging from a single unit to small quantity orders. Our efficient processes allow us to manufacture products without expensive tooling and assembly line setup costs. We guarantee that your products are manufactured to your specifications and quality standards.

TiNTiC Products are distributed through our global network of dealers. We select dealers whom we can help grow market share and increase product sales. We also select dealers who will positively represent our products. Please review our product pages to find a dealer.

We comply with all intellectual property laws. We guarantee that your interactions with us are confidential and protected. We want to build a long-term business relationship with you and we understand that this will only happen when we prioritize your objectives before our own. We look forward to working with you.
